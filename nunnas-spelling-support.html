How to set up

1
Open your Google Sheet. Go to File → Share → Publish to web. Choose the vocabulary tab, choose Comma-separated values (.csv), click Publish and copy the URL.
2
Paste the URL below and click Test. Then do the same for your false_friends tab and paste that URL too.
3
Choose your display settings and timer options, then click Generate student link. Share that link — students see only the games, no teacher settings.
